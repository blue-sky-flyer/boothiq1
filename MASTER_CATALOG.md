## Key Insights from Analysis

### 1. Service Costs Dominate Out-of-Town Projects
- Local projects: 25-35% services
- Out-of-town: 60-75% services
- Travel, per diem, and logistics drive costs significantly

### 2. Material Premiums Are Significant
- Oak vs MDF: 50% premium
- Premium flooring: 3-5x standard carpet
- Experiential props: 25-35% of total budget

### 3. Union Venues Have Higher Labor Costs
- MTCC requires show services package
- eBay project at MTCC had ~50% services (but was a reuse project)

### 4. Reuse Dramatically Reduces Costs
- eBay project: $55/sq ft (reusing AGAM frames)
- New build: $260-400/sq ft
- Reprint graphics vs new: 40-60% savings

### 5. Mobile Tours Have Unique Cost Structures
- Rental + transport: 60% of budget
- Per-activation-day: $8,850 average
- Driver/technician included in transport

## Recommendations for Quote Generation

1. **Use location-specific pricing** when available; Toronto has the most data
2. **Budget 30-35% for services** on standard Toronto builds
3. **Experiential props** should be budgeted at 25-35% if featured
4. **Out-of-town projects** - refer to CCM Hockey for observed Montreal costs (71.8% services)
5. **Reuse existing assets** where possible - 40-60% savings on graphics
6. **Add contingency** for:
   - Rush orders
   - Q4/busy season
   - Union venues (observed: higher labor costs at MTCC)
   - Non-standard hours (observed: Pride festival had premium timing)
